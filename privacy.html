Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 14, 2026

1. Data Collection

Habeeto is designed with privacy in mind. Your personal data — including habits, daily entries, goals, achievements, and settings — is stored locally on your device. We have no access to this data.

We collect anonymous, aggregated analytics data to help us understand how the app is used and improve the experience. This data cannot be used to identify you personally. See Section 4 for details.

2. Information Stored on Your Device

The following information is stored locally on your device only:

  • Habit definitions and tracking entries
  • Daily check-in records
  • Goal configurations and progress
  • Achievement and streak data
  • App settings and preferences (display name, notification time, theme, language)
  • Profile photo (if you choose to set one)

None of this information leaves your device unless you explicitly use the Backup & Restore feature, which saves data to a location you choose.

3. Subscriptions and Payments

Habeeto offers optional premium subscriptions (weekly, monthly, and yearly plans). All payments are processed entirely by Apple through the App Store. We do not collect, store, or have access to your payment information, billing details, or Apple ID.

Your subscription status is stored locally on your device to unlock premium features.

4. Analytics

Habeeto collects anonymous, aggregated usage data to help us improve the app. This may include:

  • General app usage patterns (e.g., which features are used most)
  • App performance data (e.g., crash reports, load times)
  • Device type and operating system version

This data is anonymous and cannot be linked to you personally. We do not collect your name, email, habits, or any content you create in the app. We do not build personal profiles or sell data to third parties.

Habeeto does not include any advertising frameworks or ad tracking tools.

5. Notifications

If you enable reminders, notifications are scheduled locally on your device. No notification data is sent to any external server.

6. Third-Party Services

Habeeto does not sell or share your personal data with any third parties. The app does not contain advertisements.

The app interacts with the following external services:

  • Apple App Store: For subscription verification, handled by Apple's StoreKit framework.
  • Analytics provider: For anonymous, aggregated usage data as described in Section 4.

7. Children's Privacy

Habeeto does not knowingly collect any personal information from children. The only data transmitted externally is anonymous analytics as described in Section 4. No personal data, habit content, or identifiable information is ever sent from the app.

8. Data Deletion

Since all personal data is stored locally on your device, you can delete all Habeeto data at any time by deleting the app. Previously collected anonymous analytics data cannot be linked back to you or your device.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in the "Last updated" date at the top of this document. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

10. Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at:

Email: habeeto.app@gmail.com

This privacy policy is effective as of the date listed above.
